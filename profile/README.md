# Kawakawa, Inc. 🐟

A corporation casting its net in [Prosperous Universe](https://prosperousuniverse.com/) — the
real-time, single-shard space economy MMO where supply chains, not laser
beams, are how you reel in victory.

We're a school of entrepreneurs, traders, and base-builders running an internal market
so members never have to fish for fair prices on the public exchanges.
Production is our bread and butter (well, our [DW](https://handbook.apex.prosperousuniverse.com/wiki/materials/drinking-water)
and [RAT](https://handbook.apex.prosperousuniverse.com/wiki/materials/basic-rations)),
hauling is a way of life, and nobody gets thrown overboard.

## What's in this org

The repos here are the tools we use to keep the corp swimming: the internal
market site, integrations with [FIO](https://doc.fnar.net/), Discord bots,
and whatever else floats our boat.

## Want to join?

We're always trawling for new crew. Reach out in-game or hook us on
Discord — we promise no fishy onboarding.
